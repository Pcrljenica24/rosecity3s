Rose City 3’s is Portland’s first 3v3 basketball league. We aim to provide a premium basketball experience that has never been seen in Portland before.

 We are a Fiba endorsed league.
 Stats will be kept up to date weekly along with team standings.
 Access to Fiba app where stats and standings can be viewed.
 Free jerseys to all participants.
 Tournament stile championship.
 Champions will be given prize package.
 Elite and Rec divisions available.

We will be hosting a kick off event in mid May prior to the beginning of the league. Everyone is welcome to come join us celebrate our love for basketball! A few events that will be taking place at our kickoff

 Dunk contest.
 3-point shooting contest.
 Multiple 3v3 games played by a select few Elite teams.
 Concessions will be available.
